Garlic and Oil Pasta

A delicious and simple pasta dish with a garlicky twist!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti works best)
  • 4 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (plus more for pasta water)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped, optional)
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the sliced garlic and cook gently until it becomes golden (do not burn it).
  5. Add red pepper flakes (if using) and stir for a few seconds.
  6. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the garlic and oil.
  8. Pour in a little reserved pasta water and toss everything together.
  9. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  10. Remove from heat and mix in chopped parsley (optional).
  11. Serve immediately with grated Parmesan cheese on top (optional).
