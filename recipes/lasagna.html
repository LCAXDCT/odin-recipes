Lasagna

This is a simple and delicious lasagna recipe that everyone will love!

This recipe is made for thousands grandmas around the world, and it's a family favorite, you have to try it!

    Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef (or pork)
  • 2 cups (500ml) tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 400g ricotta cheese (or 2 cups béchamel sauce)
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
